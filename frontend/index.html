Project Features

Everything you need for smarter crypto analysis.

Linear Regression Model

Our model uses a time-tested linear regression algorithm to identify trends in your data and project future price values.

Easy CSV Upload

Simply upload your historical Bitcoin data in a CSV file. Our system handles the rest, no complex setup required.

Instant Price Prediction

Get an immediate price prediction in Rupees (₹) after your data is processed, allowing for timely decision-making.

How It Works

A simple, three-step process to get your prediction.

1

Prepare Your Data

Format your data into a CSV file with 'DateTime' and 'Price' columns.

2

Upload & Enter Day

Upload your file and enter the future day number you want to predict.

3

Get Your Insight

Analyze the predicted price, calculated in Rupees (₹), generated by the model.

Required CSV Format

Your CSV file must have these two columns: 'DateTime' and 'Price'.
